Notes:

This calculation uses adjustments for extreme latitudes to set fajr and isha times to the nearest location with a less extreme latitude where there is at least a four hour night between isha and fajr.

when there is less than six hours between maghrib and sunrise, these are adjusted to the nearest location with a less extreme latitude where there is at least a six hour night between maghrib and sunrise.

In this way there is a gradual change of prayer times as you go towards extreme latitudes which then allows a reasonable gap between the late night and early morning prayers to be kept.

Asr times are based on 2/3rds of the way between zuhr and maghrib
