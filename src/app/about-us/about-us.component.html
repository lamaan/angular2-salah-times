Welcome to ShariahStandards.org!

We aim to set up a community of Muslims who work together to define and publish open global standards for Islamic Law.

Any Muslim can join this community and help develop and improve the standards that we publish on this website. Join up via Meetup.com

We hope people find this useful and that these standards become widely recognised and applied.

The process of adopting these standards is up to each individual and organisation.

The standards may change over time. The content of this site are publically archived to ensure that any older standards can be located if needed (this can currently be found here).
